Giving Back with Every Step!

At Susan Waviatu Shoe Collection, we believe in the power of giving back. That’s why we have made it our mission to provide shoes to those in need, especially children in orphanages and underserved communities.

Through our shoe trade-in program, you can donate your gently worn shoes, and in return, receive fair-priced footwear from our collection. Your contribution helps bring comfort, dignity, and joy to those who need it most.

Step forward with us—because every pair makes a difference!

Our Commitment to Giving Back

Shoe Donations

We collect gently worn shoes and donate them to orphans and those in need, helping provide comfort and dignity to underprivileged communities.

Shoe Trade-In Program

Trade in your old shoes for a fair discount on a new pair! Your contribution supports those less fortunate while you refresh your collection affordably.

Community Outreach

Our mission extends beyond shoes—we actively engage in charity events and outreach programs to uplift and empower vulnerable communities.

Supporting Children's Homes

We partner with children's homes to provide footwear to orphans, ensuring they have comfortable, durable shoes for school and everyday life.

Sustainable Fashion

By encouraging shoe recycling and trade-ins, we promote sustainability, reducing waste while ensuring quality footwear reaches those who need it most.

Join Us in Giving Back

Want to make a difference? Donate shoes, participate in our outreach programs, or volunteer with us to spread kindness—one step at a time.
